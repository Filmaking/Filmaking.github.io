8 Quick Steps to Create a Wonderful Little Film

  • Create a story
  • Choose the roles
  • Plan in which order you´re going to film the different scenes
  • Arrange the scenario and the needed equipment
  • Film the different scenes
  • Edit and assembly the film
  • Verify and publish the film (optional)
  • And most importantly, work as a team!
